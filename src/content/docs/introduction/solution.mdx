title: "2.3 Affine Solution"
---

To close the gap identified in [§2.2](/introduction/problem/), Affine operates as a production loop with three tightly coupled components:

- **Public Renewable Environment Substrate ([§5](/environments/design-criteria/)).** A curated suite of RL environments that covers software engineering, browser-grounded interaction, memory, tool orchestration, strategic games, distributional alignment, etc. The suite continuously regenerates tasks in the air and serves as both training substrate and evaluation surface.
- **Permissionless Miner Participation.** Any operator can retrieve the base checkpoint, run RL post-training, release the resulting model publicly on HuggingFace and compatible endpoints, and commit the revision on-chain to be evaluated.
- **Transparent Scoring and Settlement ([§3](/mechanism/participants/)).** These commitments and their per-task outcomes are fed to an open multi-environment scoring layer on top of a Champion Challenge pipeline. Pareto filtering permanently terminates dominated miners, and the reigning champion keeps all emission weight until a challenger wins enough consecutive checkpoints to take the crown. The resulting single-recipient weight vector is then published on Bittensor Subnet 120.

Early empirical evidence proves consistency with the design. Three independently trained miner models have exceeded the unmodified Qwen3-32B base on benchmarks not used during training, with gains of up to +14% on MCP-Bench task completion, +51% on MemoryAgentBench F1, and a score of 12.28 on SWE-rebench where the base scores zero. Measured across independent miners and benchmarks, these gains are exactly the signal Affine is built to produce: transparently incentivize verified improvements from permissonless contributiors, pushing the open ecosystem past the closed-source frontier through compounding releases.
