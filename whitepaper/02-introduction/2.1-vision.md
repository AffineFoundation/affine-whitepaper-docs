# 2.1 Vision

Affine's mission is to produce successive generations of stronger open-source models by building high-quality, verifiable, and reusable RL environments. With the environments Affine can organize a network of miners running RL post-training on top of the existing open-source base. We believe the real bottleneck in open-model progress is not model architecture, but whether the environments miners train against are realistic, hard, and measurable enough to force the model to learn capabilities that matter.

Environment construction is the engine of the progress. The team and the community continuously produce new environments and improve reward mechanisms. Miners train and compete within them. A unified multi-dimensional evaluation system then assesses model improvements across environments and benchmarks and settles incentive accordingly. Through this loop, we aim to deliver models that measurably exceed the existing open-source baselines in agentic scenarios (SWE, browser interaction, memory, etc.), and to turn open competition into a compounding process of capability growth instead of a series of one-off releases.

Affine is not a leaderboard, nor a closed lab, nor a single-model project. It is long-running public infrastructure for open reasoning progress, centered on environments that evolve through iterations of real permissonless competition.
